Privacy Policy for Arrow Puzzle

Last updated: 26 June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Arrow Puzzle" mobile game ("the App", "we", "us") handles your information. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Arrow Puzzle is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, phone number, or location) to our own servers.

All your game progress — levels completed, stars earned, streaks, and settings — is stored only on your own device using local storage. We cannot access this data.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

  • Level progress and star ratings
  • Daily challenge streaks
  • Hint count
  • Sound, vibration, and other settings

This data never leaves your device and is deleted if you uninstall the App or use the "Reset All Progress" option.

3. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services in current or future versions. These services may collect information as described in their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services — required for installing and running the App. See the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Games (optional, if you sign in) — for leaderboards. See the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob (if advertising is enabled in a future version) — may use device identifiers to serve ads. See the AdMob Privacy & Security page.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is rated suitable for everyone, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App does not require any account or sign-in to play.

5. Permissions

The App may request the Vibration permission to provide haptic feedback during gameplay. This is optional and can be disabled in the App's settings. No other sensitive permissions are required.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
